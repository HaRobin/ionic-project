About About Gaming Accessories Manager

Welcome to the Gaming Accessories Manager! This app helps you organize and manage your collection of gaming peripherals such as mice, keyboards, headsets, and other accessories. Whether you're an enthusiast or a professional gamer, this app is designed to keep track of all your gear in one place.

Features
  • Create, view, and edit your accessory details
  • Organized categories for easy browsing
  • Native mobile functionalities like photo upload
  • Firebase integration for secure and real-time data management
